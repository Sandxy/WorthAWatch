Worth a Read?

Is this blog worth reading?
That totally depends on you! If you are just half as interested in movies or tv-shows as I am, then this blog might be worth your time!
On this blog I will be rating movies and tv-shows that I have watched. I am on the search for the creepiest, funniest, most heartbreaking, darkest, most romantic or even most disturbing movies and shows of all time. Join me on that journey!
If you're interested in my opinion about your favorite movie or any other movie out there, your suggestions are more than welcome!

Lastly: Please be aware that I this is my own opinion and I am in no way telling you what to watch or what not to - I just want to share my thoughts!
