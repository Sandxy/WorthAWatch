Review: The Thing
August 18, 2022

The Thing
Horror/Sci-Fi | 1982 | 1h 49min
Director: John Carpenter

About the movie

A research team on Antarctica makes a weird find. An alien which has been frozen there for centuries, now comes to live. With that, the horror begins as the Alien being starts to take them down one by one. But when they find out, that the alien can take over the body of everyone, they can’t even trust each other anymore…

What I think

There is not that much to say about that movie. It’s a fairly predictable movie. The alien takes out the survivors one by one; nothing excitingly unique about that. It was a pretty weird movie. Like, whoever came up with the look of this thing, I would have never thought of such a weird creature. It was okay overall, not too boring, but also not as exciting. Some scenes were pretty disgusting because of all that slime, and what on earth had the director against dogs? I have never seen so many dogs killed in a movie. 

Do not recommend this movie to any dog lover – they will hate you for it.

Plot: 5/10
Originality: 4/10
Cinematogrophy: 1/10
Acting: 4/10
Entertainment: 5/10
Rewatchability: 3/10
Overall: 3,7/10
