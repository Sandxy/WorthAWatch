Review: Your Reality
September 4, 2022

Your Reality
Short/Thriller | 2019 | 21min
Director: Top Tarasin

About the movie

Alicia and Mark fall in love. They move in together and live a happy and loving live together. That's at least what the rest of the world sees. In reality, though, Mark is a controlling and gaslighting monster that has destroyed Alicia's self-esteem and self-respect piece by piece.

What I think

Amazing short film on Gaslighting. The plot might not have been as exciting as others. But the message this short film tries to tell is a very important one, and Top Tarasin, the director, did a great job of wrapping that message up in a 21 minutes long short film. I was quite positively surprised by the acting performances as well. 

Plot: 7/10
Originality: 8/10
Cinematogrophy: 6/10
Acting: 7/10
Entertainment: 8/10
Rewatchability: 6/10
Overall: 7/10
