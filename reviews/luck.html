Review: Luck
August 18, 2022

Luck
Comedy/Family | 2022 | 1h 37min
Director: Peggy Holmes

About the movie

Sam, one of the unluckiest humans to ever exist, finds her way into the world of luck itself. On the search to find some luck, so she can finally end her unlucky days, she turns the world of luck upside down.

What I think

I absolutely enjoyed every minute of the movie and didn’t find it boring at any point throughout the whole thing. The story was catching and very exciting, and so were the characters. Yes, a kid would definitely enjoy it more, but it was a very nice and cute movie, that even got my eyes to tear up at some parts. Absolutely heartwarming movie, and not just for the little ones!

Plot: 8/10
Originality: 7/10
Cinematogrophy: 6/10
Acting: 7/10
Entertainment: 10/10
Rewatchability: 9/10
Overall: 7,8/10
