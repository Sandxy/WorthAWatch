Review: Halloween
August 18, 2022

Halloween
Horror/Thriller | 1978 | 1h 32min
Director: John Carpenter

About the movie

Michael Myers, locked up in a mental hospital after the murder of his sister at age 6, escapes fifteen years later, and haunts his childhood town for new victims on the scariest night of the year: Halloween.

What I think

Although the effects and cinematography over all weren’t as impressive as they are in other movies, the sound effects were outstanding. Alone, the music got my heartbeat up quite a bit. I was tense and paranoid throughout the whole movie and some jump scares really caught me off guard. Even with a rather predictable plot, I wasn’t bored with the sight light bit.

An absolute classic that everyone should have at least watched once.

Plot: 8/10
Originality: 7/10
Cinematogrophy: 7/10
Acting: 7/10
Entertainment: 9/10
Rewatchability: 8/10
Overall: 7,7/10
