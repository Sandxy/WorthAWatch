Review: Let the Right One In
August 18, 2022

Let the Right One In
Horror/Romance | 2008 | 1h 54min
Director: Tomas Alfredson

About the movie

The defenseless and weak little Oskar is the perfect bully-target for his classmates. But with the help of his new neighbor, a girl with the name Eli, he finally stands up to them. Soon enough, though, he has to find out what dark secret Eli has to carry around with her.

What I think

That was one boring movie. It’s not a horror movie; it’s a kids-romance movie! I didn’t get caught in the story at all. Can’t really say more about it. I had a hard time getting through the whole thing, but I just couldn't shake the feeling that there had to be more. I was waiting for the turning point, but after the credits started playing, I realized that there wasn’t going to be one.

Plot: 4/10
Originality: 4/10
Cinematogrophy: 3/10
Acting: 5/10
Entertainment: 1/10
Rewatchability: 1/10
Overall: 3/10
