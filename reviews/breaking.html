Review: Breaking
September 20, 2022

Breaking
Thriller/Drama | 2022 | 1h 43min
Director: Abi Damaris Corbin

About the movie

This movie is based on true events.

Former veteran Brian Brown-Easley is denied his payment by the veterans administration. This causes him to become homeless and he decides to take some drastic measures. He walks into a bank and claims that he will blow the whole place up, if he doesn’t get the money, to which he is entitled. He also wants to be heard by everyone and tell the story of how unfair the system is.

What I think

Even though the setting of the movie didn’t change much and the location pretty much always stayed the same, it managed to keep me entertained regardless of that. The acting performances are also worth mentioning because they were incredible. Especially John Boyega, portraying Brian Brown-Eslay, delivered an astonishing performance. The ending was not only shocking, but also heartbreaking, especially considering that it is based on a real life story.

Plot: 9/10
Originality: 8/10
Cinematogrophy: 7/10
Acting: 8/10
Entertainment: 8/10
Rewatchability: 8/10
Overall: 8/10
