Review: The Disappearence of Willie Bingham
August 28, 2022

The Disappearence of Willie Bingham
Short/Horror | 2015 | 12min
Director: Matthew Richards

About the movie

Willie Bingham, who murdered a little girl, was in prison. The family of the victim had control over his body, meaning they could let body parts of Willie get surgically removed. This was introduced as a new form of punishment for criminals who have committed a felony of a certain kind. As time goes by, Willie loses more and more parts of him, and not only from his body, but also from his mind.

What I think

That short film was super dark. The plot was absolutely horrifying and it was so hard to watch because the actor Kevin Dee did such a great job and made the story really believable.
Great but absolutely terrifying short film and definitely nothing for weak nerves.

Plot: 8/10
Originality: 8/10
Cinematogrophy: 4/10
Acting: 7/10
Entertainment: 8/10
Rewatchability: 7/10
Overall: 7/10
