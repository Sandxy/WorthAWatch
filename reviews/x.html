Review: X
August 18, 2022

X
Horror/Thriller | 2022 | 1h 45min
Director: Ti West

About the movie

A small film - crew is filming their movie on an old farm. The movie is supposed to be an adult movie, which wreaks the interest of their hosts, an elderly couple. But once that sexual passion has awoken, it soon turns murderous.

What I think

Prepare yourselves for some sex scenes when you are watching this movie. I mean, obviously every horror movie has at least one sex scene, but this movie overdoes it a little. However, there weren’t only those scenes, but also some tense ones too! I absolutely loved the cross-cutting scenes and they definitely helped to build my tension up as well.

I found the story - besides being pretty disturbing - quite wicked as I would have never suspected the purpose of why they did it. I mean, an old couple turning into crazy, murderous people just to feed their sexual needs? Pretty cool idea actually. But it wasn’t executed very well. The tension of the movie got cut back quite a lot due to all the sex scenes and at some points the movie just got too dry and boring.

Plot: 7/10
Originality: 6/10
Cinematogrophy: 5/10
Acting: 8/10
Entertainment: 6/10
Rewatchability: 4/10
Overall: 6/10
