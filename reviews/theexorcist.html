Review: The Exorcist
August 18, 2022

The Exorcist
Horror/Thriller | 1973 | 2h 12min
Director: William Friedkin

About the movie

As Regan begins to show bizarre and rather worrying behavior, her mother takes her to the doctor. But neither physically nor psychologically seems to be anything wrong with the little girl. It is the priest that can tell what is wrong with her: she is possessed by the devil.

What I think

The first 40 minutes had me really worried that I wasn’t going to be able to watch the whole movie because they were SO boring and slow paced. Nothing interesting happened, and I understand that a movie doesn’t just start at its climax, but this story was dragged out way too much and was boring. On the other hand, the rest of the movie was more exciting and didn’t bore me out. Some scenes were pretty disgusting and disturbing as well. Considering I normally don’t really enjoy watching older movies, this one was quite to my liking.

Plot: 8/10
Originality: 8/10
Cinematogrophy: 4/10
Acting: 8/10
Entertainment: 7/10
Rewatchability: 5/10
Overall: 6,7/10
