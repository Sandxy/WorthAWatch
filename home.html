Review: Fall
After the death of her boyfriend Dan, due to a rock-climbing-accident, Beckys life was shattered. One year after her big loss, her best friend Hunter comes to town again. To take Becky’s mind off of things a little bit, Hunter wants to go on one of their adrenaline adventures, they always went to, before Dan’s tragic death. For this adventure, Hunter chose the 2000 feet tall B67TV tower. Together they drive out in the middle of nowhere, where the tower is located, and climb to the top of it.
