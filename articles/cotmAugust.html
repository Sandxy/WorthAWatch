Character of the Month August
Tricia Albright from Orphan: First Kill
August 2, 2022

Tricia Albright, portrayed by Julia Stiles

The mother of a child that had gone missing. She is a loving and caring person, who still grieves about her lost daughter Esther. But that is all just a facade, a big lie behind which the true Tricia Albright hides. A stone-cold person with no feelings of guilt whatsoever. When her son Gunnar accidentally killed his sister Esther, Tricia didn’t hesitate to cover up the murder and sell it as a missing child case. Throughout the movie Orphan: First kill it gets clear that Tricia does not know any boundaries or limits when it comes to protecting her family.

Why is this my Character of the Month?

Because of the way she acted and pretended to not know who Leena was. I mean sure, she didn’t exactly know who the person pretending to be her daughter was, but she knew that Leena, the person she took into her home and acted as if she’d care about her, wasn't her daughter. It not only caught Leena off guard, but also myself. The way she was so apathetic about the fact that her son killed her daughter and she covered the whole thing up was just amazing. And still, even though she looked like she had no feelings at all, she cared about her husband. Because she did everything just so he wouldn’t find out about the truth. I found her character to be very interesting and also pretty scary.
