Greatest Villains from Movies I have watched
September 16, 2022

Lord Voldemort

From “Harry Potter”

The greatest and most powerful Wizards and Witches are so scared of him, that they don’t even say his name out loud, but they call him You-Know-Who or He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named. By his followers, the Death-Eaters, he also frequently gets called the Dark Lord. And having no nose and such a pale skin tone, adds up to a pretty scary look and appearance overall.

Hannibal Lecter

A serial killer who not only kills his victims, but also eats them. He is a highly intelligent and calm man, who does not lose his temper easily. That leads to his appearance being rather sociopathic, because he doesn’t even flinch when killing - or eating - a person.

Jigsaw

From the “Saw” movies

John Kramer who is dying from an inoperable frontal lobe tumor, found his meaning in life again, by testing other peoples willpower to live. His crucial testing methods force people to go through unimaginable pain and suffering. If they are willing to do that, they live - if not, they die.

Norman Bates

From “Psycho”

Norman Bates, a young man who suffers from a split personality. One of his personalities can be sweet and kind, but if the other one takes over, also known as “Mother”, Norman brutally kills a young woman.

Michael Myers

At the age of six Michael killed his little sister and his parents had to admit him to a psychiatric clinic, where he was held for the next fifteen years. But after that time, Michael breaks out of the clinic and hunts down the rest of his family to finish the job he started with the killing of his sister.
